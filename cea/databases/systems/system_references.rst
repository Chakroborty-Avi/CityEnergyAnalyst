References for supply technologies
===================================

:Author: Shanshan Hsieh, Sreepathi Bhargava Krishna
:Date: 07 Aug 2017

Data about technology operation and cost of energy supply systems are stored
in ``..cea/databases/systems/supply_systems.xls``. This document provides references of information in the spreadsheet.


+-----------------+--------------------------------------------------------------------------+
|Column Name      | Description                                                              |
+=================+==========================================================================+
|description      | description of the technology                                            |
+-----------------+--------------------------------------------------------------------------+
|code             | unique code of the technology                                            |
|-----------------+--------------------------------------------------------------------------+
|cap_min/ cap_max | capacity range of the technology                                         |
|-----------------+--------------------------------------------------------------------------+
|unit             | unit of the capacity range, eithe in W, m2 or m3                         |
|-----------------+--------------------------------------------------------------------------+
|currency         | currency of the cost correlation                                         |
+-----------------+--------------------------------------------------------------------------+
|a,b,c,d,e,f,g    | constants in the cost correlation equation of the investment cost        |
+-----------------+--------------------------------------------------------------------------+
|LT_yr            | lifetime of the technology in years                                      |
+-----------------+--------------------------------------------------------------------------+
|O&M_%            | fixed operation and maintenance cost as a percentage of the capital cost |
+-----------------+--------------------------------------------------------------------------+
|IR_%             | interest rate of the technology                                          |
+-----------------+--------------------------------------------------------------------------+
|assumptions      | names of columns which the values are assumed                            |
+-----------------+--------------------------------------------------------------------------+
